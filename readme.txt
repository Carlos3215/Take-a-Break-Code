Take a Break Extension
This extension shows a message of "Take a Break" after every 30 minutes of work.

Installation
Open the Chrome Web Store.
Search for "Take a Break".
Click on the "Add to Chrome" button.
Click on the "Add extension" button.
Usage
The extension will automatically start working after it is installed. After every 30 minutes of work, a message will appear that says "Take a break!". You can click on the message to close it.

Settings
You can change the interval at which the extension shows a message by clicking on the extension's icon in the Chrome toolbar. The default interval is 30 minutes.

Troubleshooting
If you are having problems with the extension, you can try the following:

Make sure that you have the latest version of the extension.
Restart your computer.
Clear your browser's cache and cookies.
Uninstall and reinstall the extension.
If you are still having problems, you can contact the extension's developer for help.
